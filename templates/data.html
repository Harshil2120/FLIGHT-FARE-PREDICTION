Month vs Price

The average fare price is highest in the month of March , this can be because people usually book their fights 2-3 months prior to their date of journey which leads to higher demand and hence higher fare prices.





Day vs Price

We see that prices are highest on a friday, that may be because this is when airlines start raising prices to make up for the lower prices before and also because weekdays are off for most people so they are mostly travelling to or travelling back





Stops vs Price

As a direct/non-stop flight is accounting for fare of only one flight for a trip, its average fair is the least. As the no. of stops increase, the fare price goes up accounting for no. of flights and due to other resources being used up for the same.
